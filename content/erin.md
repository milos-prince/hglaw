Erin obtained her Bachelor of Arts in Political Studies (2016) and Master of Arts in Women’s and Gender Studies (2020) from the University of Saskatchewan. She then went on to obtain her law degree from Thompson Rivers University in Kamloops, BC in 2023. Following law school, she completed articles with the Crown Prosecutor’s Office in La Ronge, SK and was called to the Saskatchewan Bar in June 2024. Erin joined Hnatyshyn Gough in 2025 where she maintains a general practice with particular interest in family law and employment law. In her spare time, she enjoys reading, travelling, and spoiling her cats.
